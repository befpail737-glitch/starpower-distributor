Starpower Power Module Solutions for High-Efficiency Solar PV Inverters

LiTong provides comprehensive Starpower module solutions for solar photovoltaic (PV) inverters, ensuring high efficiency, robust performance, and reliability for both grid-tied and off-grid applications. Our expertise spans from residential string inverters to utility-scale central inverters, enabling system designers to optimize their designs for maximum energy harvest and long-term reliability.

Solution Block Diagram

Core Advantages

  • Superior Efficiency: Ultra-low switching losses with Starpower SiC modules for maximum energy conversion
  • Enhanced Thermal Performance: Excellent heat dissipation for reliable operation in harsh outdoor environments
  • Fast Switching Speeds: Dramatically reduced switching losses especially with SiC technology
  • Simplified Design: Reduced component count and board space requirements

Application Scenarios

  • Residential String Inverters: Compact, high-efficiency solutions for rooftop installations
  • Commercial String Inverters: Medium-power solutions for commercial solar arrays
  • Central Inverters: High-power solutions for utility-scale solar farms
  • Microinverters: Distributed inverter solutions for module-level optimization
  • Hybrid Inverters: Combined solar and battery storage solutions
