Starpower Power Module Solutions for Industrial Motor Drives & Control Systems

LiTong provides robust Starpower power module solutions for industrial motor drives and control systems, ensuring reliable operation in harsh environments. Our modules are engineered to withstand the demanding conditions found in manufacturing facilities, oil & gas operations, mining equipment, and other industrial applications where system uptime is critical.

Solution Block Diagram

Core Advantages

  • Industrial Reliability: Robust construction designed for harsh industrial environments
  • High Efficiency: Low switching and conduction losses for reduced energy consumption
  • Precise Control: Fast switching speeds for accurate motor speed and torque control
  • Thermal Performance: Excellent heat dissipation for continuous operation
  • Protection Features: Integrated protection for system safety and longevity

Application Scenarios

  • Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs): Adjustable speed drives for pumps, fans, and conveyor systems
  • Servo Drives: Precision motor control for robotics and CNC machinery
  • Industrial Automation: Motor control for assembly lines and automated equipment
  • Oil & Gas Equipment: Motor drives for pumps and compressors in harsh environments
  • Mining Machinery: Heavy-duty motor control for excavation and material handling
