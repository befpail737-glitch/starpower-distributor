Success Stories

For a major EV charging infrastructure provider, we provided comprehensive support from initial concept through production:

  • Product selection guidance for high-efficiency SiC modules

The result was a 3% improvement in system efficiency and a 6-month reduction in time-to-market.

Working with a leading solar inverter manufacturer, we helped optimize their platform for the next-generation market:

Industrial Motor Drive

For an industrial automation company developing next-generation motor drives, we provided specialized support:

The partnership enabled a 40% reduction in PCB size and a 15% improvement in efficiency.
