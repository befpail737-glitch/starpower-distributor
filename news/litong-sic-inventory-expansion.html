LiTong Expands Inventory of Starpower SiC Modules to Meet Growing EV Demand

To support the rapidly growing EV market, LiTong Electronics today announced a significant expansion of its Starpower SiC module inventory, ensuring prompt supply for automotive clients. This strategic move positions LiTong to meet the increasing demand for high-efficiency power modules in electric vehicle applications.

Market Trends Driving Expansion

The global electric vehicle market has experienced unprecedented growth, with sales projected to exceed 20 million units annually by 2025. This surge in demand has created a corresponding need for high-performance SiC power modules that enable the efficiency and fast charging capabilities essential to modern EV designs.

"We're seeing tremendous growth in EV-related inquiries and orders for Starpower SiC modules," said LiTong's CEO. "Our inventory expansion ensures we can meet this demand with prompt delivery and consistent supply for our automotive clients."

Supply Chain Considerations

The expansion addresses several critical supply chain challenges in the automotive power electronics sector:

  • Lead Time Reduction: Increased inventory enables same-day shipping for standard quantities of popular SiC modules
  • Volume Support: Enhanced stock levels accommodate large-volume orders for automotive production runs
  • Allocation Management: Strategic inventory positioning ensures fair allocation during supply constraints
  • Customer Confidence: Reliable supply enhances customer confidence in long-term program commitments

Product Range Enhancement

The expanded inventory focuses on Starpower's latest SiC MOSFET modules, including:

  • GCM300M12K-M1 Series: 1200V, 300A SiC MOSFET modules optimized for EV traction inverters
  • GCM650M12K Series: 1200V, 650A SiC modules for high-power EV charging applications
  • GCM100M12 Series: Compact 1200V, 100A SiC modules for onboard charger applications

These modules offer the high efficiency and fast switching speeds essential for EV applications, enabling designers to achieve the performance targets demanded by today's market.

Customer Benefits

The inventory expansion delivers several key benefits to LiTong's automotive customers:

  • Reduced Risk: Enhanced inventory depth reduces supply chain risk for critical automotive programs
  • Accelerated Development: Immediate availability of development quantities speeds prototyping and evaluation
  • Production Support: Reliable supply ensures uninterrupted production ramp for EV programs
  • Cost Optimization: Volume purchasing enables competitive pricing for high-volume automotive applications

Future Outlook

LiTong plans to continue monitoring market trends and adjusting inventory levels to meet evolving customer needs. The company is also investing in technical support resources to assist customers with SiC module implementation in EV applications.

"As the EV market continues to evolve, we're committed to being a reliable partner for automotive customers," added the CEO. "Our inventory expansion is just one aspect of our comprehensive support for the transition to electric mobility."

Technical Support

In addition to expanded inventory, LiTong is enhancing its technical support capabilities for SiC module applications:

  • Specialized FAE Team: Dedicated automotive application engineers with EV-specific expertise
  • Design Resources: Enhanced reference designs and application notes for EV applications
  • Training Programs: Customer training sessions on SiC module implementation and optimization
  • Development Support: Comprehensive support for prototyping and production ramp phases

Conclusion

The expansion of Starpower SiC module inventory demonstrates LiTong's commitment to supporting the growing EV market with reliable supply and expert technical support. As automotive customers increasingly turn to SiC technology for its efficiency advantages, LiTong is positioned to be their trusted partner for successful implementation.
