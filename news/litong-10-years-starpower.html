LiTong Celebrates 10 Years of Partnership with Starpower Semiconductor

A decade of innovation, growth, and partnership in power electronics that has shaped the future of energy conversion technology.

LiTong Electronics Team

August 15, 2025 • 12 min read

Today marks a significant milestone in the power electronics industry as LiTong Electronics proudly celebrates a decade of successful partnership with Starpower Semiconductor. This remarkable journey has not only transformed our company but has also contributed significantly to the advancement of power conversion technology worldwide.

A Decade of Transformative Growth

Our partnership with Starpower Semiconductor began in 2015 during a pivotal moment in the power electronics industry. The rapid adoption of renewable energy systems, the emergence of electric vehicles, and the increasing demand for energy-efficient solutions created unprecedented opportunities for innovation.

What started as a regional distribution agreement has evolved into a comprehensive strategic partnership that encompasses the entire Starpower product portfolio. From humble beginnings with a limited range of IGBT modules, we now represent Starpower's complete lineup of cutting-edge power semiconductor solutions.

The Foundation of Success

Our partnership was built on shared values of technical excellence, customer-centric innovation, and a commitment to advancing power electronics technology. This foundation has enabled us to navigate industry challenges and capitalize on emerging opportunities together.

Industry Leadership and Innovation

Throughout this decade, we have been at the forefront of supporting major industry transitions. Our partnership with Starpower has enabled us to provide cutting-edge solutions for:

Electric Vehicle Revolution

The rapid adoption of electric vehicles has been one of the most significant technology shifts of our time. Starpower's SiC modules have been instrumental in this transformation, enabling:

  • High-Efficiency Traction Inverters: Supporting OEMs in developing compact, efficient powertrains
  • Fast-Charging Infrastructure: Enabling DC fast chargers with power levels up to 350kW
  • On-Board Chargers: Compact, efficient charging solutions for vehicle integration
  • Energy Recovery Systems: Advanced regenerative braking solutions for extended range

Renewable Energy Expansion

The global transition to renewable energy has created unprecedented demand for efficient power conversion solutions. Our partnership has supported:

  • Solar Inverter Optimization: High-efficiency string and central inverters for residential and utility-scale applications
  • Wind Power Conversion: Robust solutions for variable-speed wind turbine systems
  • Energy Storage Systems: Bidirectional converters for battery energy storage applications
  • Smart Grid Integration: Advanced power electronics for grid-tied renewable energy systems

Industrial Automation Advancement

The evolution of industrial automation has demanded increasingly sophisticated power solutions. We have supported customers with:

  • Variable Frequency Drives: High-performance motor control solutions for industrial applications
  • Robotics and Automation: Precision motion control systems for manufacturing
  • Process Control Systems: Reliable power solutions for critical industrial processes
  • Smart Manufacturing: Integrated solutions for Industry 4.0 applications

Technical Excellence and Support

Our commitment to technical excellence has been a cornerstone of our partnership. We have invested significantly in building a world-class technical support infrastructure:

Field Application Engineering

Our FAE team has grown from 3 specialists to over 15 engineers with deep expertise across multiple application domains. This team provides:

  • Design Consultation: Early-stage design guidance and optimization
  • Application Notes: Comprehensive technical documentation and best practices
  • Prototype Support: Hands-on assistance with initial design validation
  • Troubleshooting: Expert support for complex technical challenges

Testing and Validation

Our technical center features state-of-the-art testing equipment for comprehensive validation:

  • Thermal Testing: Advanced thermal characterization and analysis
  • Electrical Performance: Comprehensive electrical testing and validation
  • Reliability Testing: Accelerated life testing and reliability analysis
  • Application Simulation: System-level simulation and modeling support

Global Reach and Local Expertise

Our global presence has expanded significantly over the past decade, enabling us to serve customers worldwide while maintaining local expertise and support. We now operate in 30+ countries with regional offices and technical support centers strategically located to serve key markets.

This global-local approach ensures that customers receive both international expertise and localized support tailored to their specific market requirements and regulatory environments.

Looking Ahead: The Next Decade

As we celebrate this milestone, we are excited about the opportunities that lie ahead. The power electronics industry is entering a new era of innovation driven by:

Emerging Technologies

  • Wide Bandgap Semiconductors: SiC and GaN technologies for higher efficiency and power density
  • Digital Power: Intelligent power management with advanced control algorithms
  • Integrated Solutions: Highly integrated power modules with built-in intelligence
  • Sustainable Design: Eco-friendly materials and manufacturing processes

Market Opportunities

  • Electrification Trends: Continued growth in EV, renewable energy, and industrial automation
  • Energy Efficiency: Increasing demand for high-efficiency power conversion solutions
  • Smart Infrastructure: Integration of power electronics with IoT and AI technologies
  • Global Expansion: Emerging markets and new application areas
