About LiTong Electronics

Detailed introduction to our company's history, team strength, and client case studies. We are a core distributor of Starpower Semiconductor.

We are a core distributor of Starpower Semiconductor

Founded in 2010, LiTong Electronics Co., Ltd. has established itself as a leading authorized distributor of Starpower Semiconductor power modules. With over 15 years of experience in the power electronics industry, we provide comprehensive support to engineers and procurement teams worldwide.

Our History of Cooperation with Starpower

Our partnership with Starpower Semiconductor began in 2015, marking the beginning of a fruitful collaboration that has grown stronger each year. As one of Starpower's earliest authorized distributors in our region, we have been instrumental in introducing their cutting-edge power module technologies to the local market.

Over the years, we have consistently achieved top performance metrics in sales volume, customer satisfaction, and technical support quality. Our commitment to excellence has been recognized with multiple awards from Starpower, including "Platinum Partner" status in 2020.

Client Case Studies

Our expertise spans diverse industries including automotive, renewable energy, industrial automation, and consumer electronics. We have supported projects ranging from small-scale prototypes to large-volume production runs.

Notable successes include supporting a major EV manufacturer in transitioning to SiC technology for their traction inverters, helping a solar inverter company optimize their design for higher efficiency, and assisting an industrial equipment manufacturer in reducing system costs while improving reliability.

We provide digital certificates for product tracking and customs declarations, ensuring full compliance with import regulations and quality standards.
