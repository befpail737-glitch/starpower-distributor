Our FAE team finds the GD75PIT120C5S an excellent choice for compact motor control applications, offering a superb balance of performance and integration. FAQ

What is a PIM module?

A PIM (Power Integrated Module) integrates IGBTs, diodes, and often a gate driver circuit into a single package for simplified design.

What are the main advantages of this module?

High integration, reduced board space, enhanced reliability due to internal protection features, and lower overall system cost.
