GD10PJA120L2S IGBT PIM Module

1200V, 10A Power Integrated Module with advanced protection features for compact motor control and power supply applications.

LiTong Engineering Team

Technical Review • August 25, 2025

The GD10PJA120L2S represents Starpower's commitment to integrated power solutions, combining high-performance IGBT technology with sophisticated protection circuitry in a compact, user-friendly package. This Power Integrated Module (PIM) is specifically engineered for applications requiring reliable power switching with minimal external components.

Key Features & Benefits

Integrated Protection

Built-in overcurrent protection, short-circuit protection, and thermal shutdown features ensure system reliability and safety.

Compact Design

Highly integrated package reduces PCB footprint and simplifies thermal management compared to discrete solutions.

Low Power Losses

Optimized IGBT structure and advanced packaging technology minimize conduction and switching losses.

Enhanced Thermal Performance

Low thermal resistance and high junction temperature capability enable operation in demanding environments.

Easy Integration

Integrated gate driver and protection circuitry simplify design and reduce time-to-market for new products.

Industrial Grade

Designed for harsh industrial environments with robust construction and comprehensive quality assurance.

Application Areas

Small Motor Drives

Perfect for fractional horsepower motor control in appliances, fans, and small industrial equipment. The integrated protection features ensure reliable operation in consumer and light industrial applications.

Servo Systems

Ideal for precision motion control in robotics, CNC machines, and automated manufacturing equipment where compact size and high reliability are critical.

Power Supplies

Suitable for switched-mode power supplies, DC-DC converters, and UPS systems requiring efficient power switching with integrated protection.

Renewable Energy

Applicable in small-scale solar inverters, wind turbine controllers, and other renewable energy systems where efficiency and reliability are paramount.
