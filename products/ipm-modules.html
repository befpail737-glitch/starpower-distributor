Starpower Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs)

Integrated solutions with built-in protection for simplified motor control designs

Starpower Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs) integrate power switches, gate drivers, and protection circuits into a single package, significantly simplifying the design of motor control applications. These modules are ideal for home appliances, HVAC systems, and small industrial drives where compact size and design simplicity are crucial.

As an authorized Starpower distributor, LiTong offers the complete range of IPM modules with comprehensive technical support for integration and optimization. Our FAE team provides design-in assistance and application guidance to ensure successful implementation.

Key Features of Starpower IPMs

  • Integrated Gate Drivers: Built-in high-performance gate drivers eliminate external components
  • Protection Functions: Overcurrent, overtemperature, and undervoltage lockout protection
  • Compact Design: Highly integrated packages reduce PCB space requirements
  • Easy Implementation: Simplified interface reduces design complexity and time-to-market
  • Reliability: Enhanced system reliability through integrated protection features
