FAQ

What distinguishes this module from all others in its class?

The GDM400N60L stands apart through its revolutionary RDS(on) of just 0.03Ω, representing a 25% improvement over previous-generation solutions. Its proprietary thermal interface technology achieves an unprecedented junction-to-case thermal resistance of 0.10°C/W, enabling safe operation at power levels previously thought impossible. The module also features aerospace-grade materials and construction, ensuring uncompromising reliability in the harshest environments.

How does this module enable next-generation industrial systems?

By delivering 400A at just 0.03Ω RDS(on), the GDM400N60L enables industrial systems to achieve power densities exceeding 10kW/in³ while maintaining exceptional efficiency above 99%. This breakthrough allows engineers to reduce system size by up to 40% while improving performance, fundamentally transforming what's possible in ultra-high-power applications like massive motor drives and industrial heating systems.
