Starpower GCM400M12K SiC MOSFET Module

Distributor Review/Application Interpretation

The GCM400M12K represents the pinnacle of Starpower's SiC technology for high-power applications. Our FAE team has successfully implemented this module in multiple EV charging solutions, where its low switching losses and high efficiency have significantly reduced system cooling requirements. The module's robust gate oxide reliability makes it suitable for the demanding conditions found in automotive applications.

Key Features

  • 1200V / 400A rating for high-power applications
  • Ultra-low switching losses for high efficiency

  • Industrial high-frequency power supplies

FAQ

What are the efficiency improvements compared to silicon IGBTs?

Depending on the application, the GCM400M12K can improve system efficiency by 1-3% compared to equivalent silicon IGBT solutions. In high-frequency applications, the improvement can be even more significant due to the dramatically reduced switching losses.

What gate drive requirements does this module have?

The module requires a gate drive voltage of +18V/-4V for optimal performance. The negative gate voltage is kept relatively low to minimize turn-off losses while still providing adequate noise immunity. Gate resistance should be between 1Ω and 3Ω for most applications.

How does the thermal design of this module compare to other SiC modules?

The GCM400M12K features an advanced thermal design with a junction-to-case thermal resistance of just 0.18°C/W. This is significantly better than many competing modules, allowing for higher power density and more compact system designs.
