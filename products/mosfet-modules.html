Starpower MOSFET Modules

High-frequency switching solutions for power electronics applications

Starpower MOSFET modules provide excellent solutions for applications requiring high-frequency switching, low conduction losses, and fast switching speeds. These modules are ideal for DC-DC converters, switch-mode power supplies, and other high-frequency power conversion applications.

As an authorized Starpower distributor, LiTong offers a comprehensive range of MOSFET modules with full technical support for design-in and optimization. Our team provides application guidance and selection assistance to ensure optimal performance in your specific application.

Key Features of Starpower MOSFET Modules

  • High Switching Speed: Nanosecond switching for high-frequency applications
  • Low Conduction Losses: Optimized for efficiency in power conversion
  • Low Gate Charge: Reduced drive power requirements
  • Thermal Performance: Excellent heat dissipation for reliable operation
  • Easy Parallel Operation: Designed for current sharing in high-power applications
