Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and partnership opportunities

What makes LiTong a preferred Starpower distributor?

As an authorized Starpower distributor, we offer several key advantages: guaranteed genuine products with full traceability, expert technical support from our Field Application Engineering team, competitive pricing with flexible terms, reliable supply chain with extensive inventory, and comprehensive after-sales service including warranty support and technical assistance.

How long has LiTong been partnering with Starpower?

LiTong has been an authorized Starpower distributor since 2015, making our partnership over 10 years strong. During this time, we've grown to become one of the top distributors in our region, consistently achieving performance metrics that exceed expectations.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we provide worldwide shipping with full customs documentation and tracking. Our logistics partners ensure reliable delivery to over 30 countries, with special attention to import regulations and quality standards in each region.

Are your products genuine Starpower modules?

Absolutely. As an authorized Starpower distributor, all products purchased from LiTong come with manufacturer certificates and full traceability documentation. We provide digital certificates for product tracking and customs declarations, ensuring complete authenticity verification.

How can I verify the authenticity of Starpower modules?

All products purchased from LiTong come with manufacturer certificates and full traceability documentation. We provide digital certificates for product tracking and customs declarations, ensuring complete authenticity verification. Additionally, our authorization from Starpower Semiconductor guarantees genuine products.

What is the lead time for orders?

For in-stock items, we typically ship within 1-2 business days. For standard products, lead times are generally 2-4 weeks. For high-volume or custom orders, lead times may extend to 6-8 weeks. We maintain extensive inventory to minimize delays and provide priority allocation for our key customers.

Do you offer technical documentation and support?

Absolutely. We provide complete technical documentation including datasheets, application notes, and reference designs. Our dedicated Field Application Engineering team offers design-in support, selection assistance, and troubleshooting for all Starpower products. We also host regular technical webinars and workshops.

Can I get samples for evaluation?

Yes, we offer sample requests for qualified customers. Please contact our sales team with details about your project and evaluation requirements. We typically provide 1-5 pieces for initial evaluation, with expedited shipping available for urgent needs.

What product families do you carry?

We carry the complete Starpower product portfolio including IGBT modules, SiC MOSFET modules, Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs), and discrete MOSFETs. Our inventory includes both standard and custom configurations to meet diverse application requirements.

How do I get technical support for my design?

Our Field Application Engineering team is available to assist with all technical aspects of your design. You can reach us via phone, email, or through our contact form. For complex projects, we offer on-site support and design reviews. Our technical resources include selection guides, application notes, and reference designs.

Do you provide design-in assistance?

Yes, our FAE team provides comprehensive design-in assistance including circuit design review, thermal analysis, PCB layout guidance, and EMI considerations. We can also provide SPICE models and simulation tools to help with your design process.

What training resources do you offer?

We offer a variety of training resources including technical webinars, hands-on workshops, application notes, and video tutorials. Our training covers product selection, implementation best practices, and troubleshooting techniques. Custom training sessions are also available for key customers.

Do you have SPICE models for your products?

Yes, we provide SPICE models for most Starpower products. These models are available for download from our technical support portal and can be used for circuit simulation and design optimization. Please contact our FAE team for specific model requests.

What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible payment terms based on customer relationship and order volume. Standard terms are Net 30 days for established customers. For new customers, we typically require payment in advance or Letter of Credit. Volume discounts and special pricing are available for long-term partnerships.

How do I become a partner or reseller?

We welcome qualified partners and resellers. Please contact our business development team with information about your company, target markets, and partnership goals. We evaluate potential partners based on technical capability, market reach, and commitment to customer service. Approved partners receive special pricing, marketing support, and training.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, we offer volume discounts based on annual purchase commitments. Discounts increase with volume, with special pricing available for strategic partnerships. Please contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements and receive a customized quotation.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns for unused products in original packaging within 30 days of purchase. A restocking fee of 15% applies to all returns. Defective products are covered by our warranty policy and can be returned for replacement or credit. Please contact our customer service team for return authorization.

Do you offer custom packaging or labeling?

Yes, we offer custom packaging and labeling services for qualified customers with volume commitments. This includes custom part numbering, branding, and special handling instructions. Please contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements.

What is your warranty policy?

Starpower products purchased from LiTong come with a standard 12-month warranty from date of purchase. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use and conditions. The warranty does not cover damage caused by misuse, improper installation, or unauthorized modifications.

How do I submit a warranty claim?

To submit a warranty claim, please contact our customer service team with your order number, part number, and description of the issue. We will provide a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and instructions for returning the product. Claims are typically processed within 5 business days of receipt.

Do you provide repair services?

We provide repair services for out-of-warranty products and certain custom assemblies. Repair services are subject to evaluation and quotation. For standard modules, replacement is typically more cost-effective than repair. Contact our service team for evaluation and pricing.

What technical support is available after purchase?

We provide ongoing technical support for all customers, including application assistance, troubleshooting, and design guidance. Our FAE team is available to help with implementation challenges, optimization opportunities, and system-level integration issues throughout the product lifecycle.
