William Taylor

Senior FAE

William Taylor is a Senior FAE at LiTong Electronics with specialized expertise in automotive applications and functional safety. His work focuses on AEC-Q101 qualified modules and ISO 26262 compliance for automotive systems, helping automotive suppliers implement robust and reliable power electronics solutions.

With over 12 years of experience in automotive power electronics, William has become a recognized expert in automotive qualification standards and functional safety requirements. He has played key roles in numerous successful automotive qualification projects and has extensive experience with both component-level and system-level safety assessments.
