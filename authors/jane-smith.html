Jane Smith

Senior Field Application Engineer

Jane Smith is a Senior Field Application Engineer at LiTong Electronics with over 15 years of experience in power electronics design and implementation. She specializes in IGBT and SiC module applications, helping customers optimize their designs for maximum performance and reliability.

Before joining LiTong, Jane worked at several leading power electronics companies, where she gained extensive experience in both design and application engineering roles. She holds a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from a prestigious university and is a member of IEEE Power Electronics Society.
