Jennifer Lee

Application Engineering Manager

Jennifer Lee is an Application Engineering Manager at LiTong Electronics with extensive expertise in industrial motor drives and power supply applications. Her work focuses on system reliability and cost optimization, helping customers achieve maximum value from their power electronics investments.

With over 15 years of experience in industrial power electronics, Jennifer has led numerous successful projects across diverse sectors including manufacturing automation, renewable energy, and transportation. She has earned recognition for her ability to translate complex technical challenges into practical, cost-effective solutions that deliver measurable business value.
