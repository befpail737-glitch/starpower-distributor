Michael Chen

Lead Field Application Engineer

Michael Chen is a Lead Field Application Engineer at LiTong Electronics with specialized expertise in gate drive design and electromagnetic compatibility. His work focuses on optimizing switching performance while ensuring EMC compliance in challenging environments.

With over 18 years of experience in power electronics design, Michael has become recognized as an expert in gate drive optimization and EMC design. He has authored numerous application notes and guides that are widely referenced by design engineers across multiple industries.

Areas of Expertise

  • Gate drive circuit design and optimization
  • Electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) design
  • Switching performance optimization
  • PCB layout for power electronics
  • dv/dt and di/dt effects mitigation
  • EMI filtering and shielding techniques
