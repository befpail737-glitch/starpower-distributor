David Brown

Field Application Engineer

David Brown is a Field Application Engineer at LiTong Electronics with specialized expertise in SiC technology and high-frequency applications. His work focuses on EV charging systems and renewable energy inverter designs, helping customers leverage advanced wide bandgap semiconductors for maximum performance gains.

With over 10 years of experience in SiC and GaN technologies, David has been instrumental in numerous breakthrough implementations in electric vehicle charging, solar inverters, and industrial power conversion systems. He has published extensively on wide bandgap semiconductor applications and regularly speaks at industry conferences and workshops.
