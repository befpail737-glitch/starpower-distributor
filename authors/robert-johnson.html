Robert Johnson

Principal Field Application Engineer

Robert Johnson is a Principal Field Application Engineer at LiTong Electronics with extensive expertise in thermal design and reliability engineering. His work focuses on high-power module applications and system-level thermal optimization, helping customers achieve maximum performance and longevity from their power electronics systems.

With over 20 years of experience in power electronics thermal management, Robert has contributed to numerous breakthrough designs in industrial, automotive, and renewable energy applications. He holds multiple patents related to advanced thermal interface materials and heatsink optimization techniques.

Areas of Expertise

  • Advanced thermal management for power modules
  • Reliability engineering and lifetime prediction
  • High-power converter thermal design
  • Thermal interface materials and application
  • Heatsink design and optimization
  • Thermal cycling and fatigue analysis
