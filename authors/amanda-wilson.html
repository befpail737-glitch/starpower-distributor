Amanda Wilson

Application Engineering Manager

Amanda Wilson is an Application Engineering Manager at LiTong Electronics with specialized expertise in consumer electronics and smart home applications. Her work focuses on design simplification and cost optimization, helping consumer electronics manufacturers implement advanced power electronics solutions efficiently and economically.

With over 14 years of experience in consumer electronics and smart home technologies, Amanda has become recognized for her ability to bridge the gap between advanced power electronics capabilities and practical consumer product requirements. She has led numerous successful projects that have enabled manufacturers to bring innovative products to market faster and more cost-effectively.
