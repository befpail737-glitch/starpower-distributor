Sarah Williams

Senior Application Engineer

Sarah Williams is a Senior Application Engineer at LiTong Electronics with specialized expertise in IPM modules and motor control applications. Her work focuses on smart home appliance and HVAC system implementations, helping customers leverage advanced motor control technologies for enhanced performance and energy efficiency.

With over 12 years of experience in motor control and power electronics, Sarah has become known for her ability to translate complex technical concepts into practical implementation strategies. She has extensive experience with brushless DC motor (BLDC) controls and permanent magnet synchronous motor (PMSM) drives.
